Did You Try An Indian Dish Before Now?

As we know that indian food known on the large amount of spices on it!

The most popular and best dishes you should try in India are:

  • Biriyani - Famous specialty of Bangalore

    • Biriyani is a specialty and traditional mixed rice dish that you cannot ignore on the way to discover Indian cuisine. Chef will use the sticky and fragrant Basmati rice only found in India, seasoned with saffron, herbs, onions, garlic, beans and cardamom and then lightly cooked, then cooked with chicken, goat or lamb. Biriyani is usually eaten with frozen milk and roasted eggplant for extra flavor.

people usually eat with their bare hands

    • Thali is one of the Indian dishes that tourists often encounter in this country.   • Tikka Masala chicken

    • Tikka Masala chicken is an Indian specialty dish with a gentle, warm taste that often appears in family meals. The dish is made from chicken marinated in Tikka Masala powder, unsweetened yogurt with chili powder, ginger powder, which is true to the spicy taste of most Indians. Sauce is also made from spicy spices, cream and tomato paste, creating a unique taste for the dish.
